# 💰 MoneyTrack — Personal Finance Tracker

A personal, private money-tracking app that runs entirely on your computer.
No accounts, no servers — your data stays with you and is saved automatically.

## How to open

Just double-click **`index.html`** (or right-click → Open With → Chrome/Safari).
Bookmark it for daily use.

## What it tracks

| Tab | What it's for |
|---|---|
| 📊 **Dashboard** | This month's spending, budget progress, portfolio value, total sent home, charts, recent activity |
| 🧾 **Expenses** | Daily expenses with categories (food, rent, transport, …), filter by month |
| 📈 **Investments** | Your Robinhood holdings — shares, avg cost, current price, live gain/loss and allocation chart |
| 🏠 **Sent Home** | Money transfers home — USD sent, exchange rate, ₹ received, per-recipient history |

## How your data is saved

Everything is stored automatically in your browser's local storage the moment
you add or edit anything. Close the tab, reopen tomorrow — it's all still there.

⚠️ **Important:** data lives in the browser you use. Always open the app in the
**same browser**, and don't use incognito mode. Clearing browser site data will
erase it — so use backups:

- **⬇ Export Backup** (bottom of sidebar) downloads a JSON file of everything.
  Do this weekly or after big updates.
- **⬆ Import Backup** restores from that file (also works to move to a new
  computer or browser).

## Daily workflow

1. Open the app, go to **Expenses**, log what you spent today.
2. When you invest or prices move, open **Investments** and click ✏️ on a
   holding to update its current price — gains recalculate instantly.
3. When you send money home, log it in **Sent Home** with the exchange rate
   so you can see the ₹ total too.
4. Set your **Monthly Budget** on the Dashboard to see how much you have left.
